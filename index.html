The Problem

Sound familiar?

Pay decisions feel arbitrary

Managers make offers based on gut instinct, not structure. Equity liability grows quietly in the background — and by the time it surfaces, it's expensive.

Job titles multiply without logic

No clear levels, no career ladder, no rationale for who gets paid what. Your best people are asking questions you can't answer.

You know skills matter — but have no infrastructure

Everyone is talking about skills-first talent strategy. Almost no one has built the foundation to support it.
